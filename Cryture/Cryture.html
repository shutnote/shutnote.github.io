Name: Crytures

Description: Crytures is a rougelite cryture collecting/fighting game, Heavily inspired by Pokemon. It is the current project I am working on.

Features:

Cryture

Fluff describing the implementation and design. Health is HP, Attack is damage it can do, Defence is damage reduction, Stamina is how often can attack. Cryutures can learn different moves for battle. They gain levels through battling and Adaptations.

Adaptations!

Adaptations are this games evolutions, something about changing appearnce, adaptations being different types, learning different moves. etc. The same cryture but with different adaptations will look and play different. A stone bat will take more hits than an urban bat but be much slower.

Battling other crytures is important for the game!

Fluff about the implementation and how battling works, it's not turn based but instead attacks are based on cryture stamina, high stamina = more attacks. Attacks vary in both stamina cost and attack power. Generally high damage moves cost more, resulting in oppenents getting more attacks off.

Inventory!

Fluff about player inventory and different items. Capture rods for catching, Adaptation items for gaining adaptations, healing, etc.
