Shadows of Divinity

Shadows of Divintiy is unity narrative project. Built with a custom cutscene system.

Overall this project achieved a 79%

This was a group project and I served as the programmer.

I built a cutscene system for the project that included scripted events, playable cutscenes, tutorial popups and triggers.

The system was used by my team mates to help with the production and development of the cutscenes.

The game also featured a spell system. The final submission featured a growth spell and a push, a stop spell and a passive time slow spell. Early versions of a electric spell exsisted but remained unsed.

For development, a spell framework was made for the different spells to be named, have icons and have a functions for needed parts such as on casted.
