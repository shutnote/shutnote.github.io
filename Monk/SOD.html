Name: Shadows Of Divintiy

Description: Shadows of Divintiy is aimed at being a narrative driven game developed in Unity. It was a university group project that I was the programmer for. It achieved a 79%.

Features:

This cutscene system is completely custom

Nodes are placed, these nodes can lead to other nodes for camera movemment, prop movement(Such as NPC, etc) and call functions when triggered. There are also zones that wait till a condition is meet. Used in the tutorial section. Whilst I was responsible for making some of the cutscenes my teammates also had too use the system.
